About myself

Discovering new places through travels is not only a fascinating adventure for me but also a passion that gives color and depth to my life. In 2023, I managed to fulfill my dream of visiting 17 different cities, which was an unforgettable experience for me.

I set myself an ambitious goal of traveling at least once a month, and proudly, I can say that I've achieved it! Each journey was a unique challenge that not only broadened my horizons but also developed my skills in handling unexpected situations flexibly.

Planning each journey required precision, but at the same time, I love deriving joy from the spontaneity of travel. For me, every new destination is like an undiscovered book, full of fascinating stories, ready for exploration.

Traveling is for me not only a change of scenery but also a constant broadening of horizons and personal growth. Every new step is for me a unique chapter in this extraordinary journey through life, full of fascinating experiences and inspirations.

I invite you to join me in exploring the world together!

Top 5 travels in 2023

Discovering the most captivating destinations is one of the most exciting experiences for every travel enthusiast. Among numerous journeys, here are my top 5:

Position Destination Experiences and Notes
1 London, UK London delighted me with its eclectic character - from historical landmarks, through cultural diversity, to vibrant nightlife. A stroll along the Thames, a visit to the British Museum, and an evening in Covent Garden are just a few of the countless unforgettable moments.
2 Madeira, Portugal Madeira surprised me with its beauty - picturesque cliffs, lush gardens, and a friendly atmosphere made every day a true paradise.
3 Edinburgh, UK Edinburgh is a city full of history and mysteries, and the view of the castle on the hill at sunrise and sunset is unique.
4 Crete, Greece Crete amazed me with its rich history and paradise beaches. Hiking the Samaria Gorge Trail in the White Mountains, exploring the ancient Palace of Knossos, the beaches of Gramvousa and Balos are just some of the attractions that made my stay unforgettable.
5 Rome, Italy Rome is a true open-air museum, where every corner pulses with history. The Colosseum, the Vatican, the Trevi Fountain - these icons enchanted me with the atmosphere of the Eternal City.

Rekommendations

Traveling is undoubtedly a source of unforgettable moments, inspiration, and new experiences. However, to fully enjoy travels, it's worth dedicating time to proper preparation. Here are a few practical tips for traveling:

  1. Plan ahead:
    • Organizing your travel plan will help avoid stress and time loss. Book accommodation and transportation in advance, and mark the main attractions to visit.
  2. Pack Wisely:
    • Limit the number of items you take. Check the weather forecast, make a list of essential items, and also remember chargers, a travel first aid kit, and documents.
  3. Don't Forget Documents:
    • Before the trip, check the validity of your passport, organize necessary visas, and make copies of important documents. Always keep a copy of your documents in a safe place.
  4. Explore the Local Culture:
    • Familiarize yourself with the customs and traditions of the places you intend to visit to avoid potential misunderstandings.
  5. Use Travel Apps:
    • Utilize mobile apps for easier navigation at your destination, booking tickets, or language translation.
